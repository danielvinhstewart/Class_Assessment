Asked Questions

What happens in the initial stages of a project?
At Arrow Media, our well-adjusted members of staff are often likely to leave all work until the very last minute. The initial stages of any project can be seen as procrastinating at the highest most professional level.
How often will information about the projects progress be discussed?
Depending on many macro elements (i.e. the weather outside and/or the severity of our hangover), customers can rest assured that members of Arrow Media have a 15% likelihood of contacting their customers on a monthly basis.
Who do we contact within the company if we have a question?
Please hold Arrow Media's help line on 01273 123 456. Your calls are very important to us.
How much does a typical project cost?
Project prices are calculated based off of an hourly rate of £35. A minimum fee of £90 applies to all projects.
How long does a typical project take to complete?
Completion times between projects ultimately depend on the size of the work and how much of a decent first impression you leave on us. Should we find out that you were the fry-cook that spat in our burgers 8 years ago, you can expect the same niceties and stellar performance from us.
What are the payment terms?
Terms of payment can be found in our Terms & Conditions page. Please read through every single point to ensure you are familiar with them.
What happens after the project is completed?
Unfortunately, we are only responsible for the functionality of any project whilst we are being paid. Sorry for not holding your hand the entire way through.
